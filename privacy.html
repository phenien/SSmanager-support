Your Privacy Matters: SSmanager is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username: Your chosen username for login
  • Name: Your full name for identification within the app
  • Email: Used for authentication and account recovery
  • Password: Securely encrypted and stored via Firebase Authentication
  • Role: Your assigned role (Admin, Teacher, or Viewer)

1.2 Student Information

When managing students, you may store:

  • First and last names
  • Date of birth (health & fitness data)
  • Parent/guardian contact information (name, phone number, email - optional)
  • Last confession dates (sensitive religious information - optional)
  • Notes and observations (optional)

1.3 Class and Attendance Data

We store information about:

  • Class names, age groups, and colors
  • Lesson schedules, topics, and scriptures
  • Attendance records (present, absent, excused)
  • Teacher assignments

1.4 Usage Data and Identifiers

We automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, iOS version
  • Device ID: For ad serving and analytics (only if you grant tracking permission)
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data for personalized ads (only if you grant tracking permission)
  • App Usage Analytics: Features used, crash reports for improving the app
  • Network Status: For offline mode functionality

1.5 Notification Preferences

We store your preferences for:

  • Lesson reminders
  • Student birthday alerts
  • Attendance reminders
  • New student notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

We use your information to:

  • Provide authentication and account management
  • Enable class and student management features
  • Track attendance and generate reports
  • Schedule and manage lessons
  • Display insights and analytics
  • Sync data across your devices

2.2 Notifications

Based on your preferences, we send:

  • Reminders for upcoming lessons
  • Alerts for student birthdays
  • Attendance task reminders
  • Notifications about new students

2.3 App Improvement

We analyze usage data to:

  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve app performance
  • Develop new features
  • Enhance user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Cloud Storage

Your data is securely stored using:

  • Firebase Authentication: Industry-standard secure authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Google's secure, scalable database
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest

3.2 Local Storage

For offline functionality:

  • Data is cached locally on your device
  • Local data is protected by iOS security features
  • Cache is automatically synced when online

3.3 Security Measures

We implement:

  • Secure password hashing and encryption
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular security audits
  • Automatic logout after inactivity
  • Protection against unauthorized access

5. Advertising and Tracking

5.1 App Tracking Transparency

Your Choice Matters: When you first launch SSmanager, you'll see a permission request asking if you want to allow tracking for personalized ads. You are in complete control.

What is Tracking? Tracking means linking data collected from our app with data from other companies' apps or websites for advertising purposes. This helps show you more relevant ads.

5.2 Free Version - Advertising

The free version displays ads via Google AdMob. Your choice determines what data we collect:

If You Choose "Allow" (Personalized Ads):

  • We collect your Device ID (IDFA) for ad targeting
  • We collect Advertising Data about your ad interactions
  • Ads are personalized based on your interests and activity
  • Potentially higher ad revenue helps us maintain the free version
  • Ads are shown at the bottom of screens

If You Choose "Ask App Not to Track" (Non-Personalized Ads):

  • We do NOT collect your Device ID or Advertising Data
  • Ads are shown but NOT personalized to you
  • Ads are contextual only (based on app content, not your behavior)
  • Your privacy is maximized while keeping the app free
  • Ads are shown at the bottom of screens

Third-Party Ad Provider:

  • Google AdMob serves all advertisements
  • AdMob's data practices depend on your tracking choice
  • View Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.3 Premium Version (Ad-Free)

Premium users enjoy:

  • Completely ad-free experience
  • No tracking - We don't collect Device ID or Advertising Data
  • No ad-related data collection whatsoever
  • One-time purchase (£3.99)
  • Support the development of SSmanager
