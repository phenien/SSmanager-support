Your Privacy Matters: SSmanager is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username: Your chosen username for login
  • Name: Your full name for identification within the app
  • Email: Used for authentication and account recovery
  • Password: Securely encrypted and stored via Firebase Authentication
  • Role: Your assigned role (Admin, Teacher, or Viewer)

1.2 Student Information

When managing students, you may store:

  • First and last names
  • Date of birth
  • Parent/guardian contact information (optional)
  • Medical information (optional)
  • Emergency contacts (optional)
  • Notes and observations (optional)

1.3 Class and Attendance Data

We store information about:

  • Class names, age groups, and colors
  • Lesson schedules, topics, and scriptures
  • Attendance records (present, absent, excused)
  • Teacher assignments

1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, iOS version)
  • App usage analytics (features used, crash reports)
  • Network status (for offline mode functionality)

1.5 Notification Preferences

We store your preferences for:

  • Lesson reminders
  • Student birthday alerts
  • Attendance reminders
  • New student notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

We use your information to:

  • Provide authentication and account management
  • Enable class and student management features
  • Track attendance and generate reports
  • Schedule and manage lessons
  • Display insights and analytics
  • Sync data across your devices

2.2 Notifications

Based on your preferences, we send:

  • Reminders for upcoming lessons
  • Alerts for student birthdays
  • Attendance task reminders
  • Notifications about new students

2.3 App Improvement

We analyze usage data to:

  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve app performance
  • Develop new features
  • Enhance user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Cloud Storage

Your data is securely stored using:

  • Firebase Authentication: Industry-standard secure authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Google's secure, scalable database
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest

3.2 Local Storage

For offline functionality:

  • Data is cached locally on your device
  • Local data is protected by iOS security features
  • Cache is automatically synced when online

3.3 Security Measures

We implement:

  • Secure password hashing and encryption
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular security audits
  • Automatic logout after inactivity
  • Protection against unauthorized access

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database hosting
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (for free users)
  • Apple StoreKit: In-app purchases

4.3 Data Export

You can export your data as:

  • PDF reports (attendance, class lists, insights)
  • CSV files (for spreadsheet import)
  • These exports are generated locally on your device

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and safety
  • Investigate fraud or security issues

5. Advertising

5.1 Free Version

The free version displays ads via Google AdMob:

  • Ads are shown at the bottom of screens
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad targeting
  • You can view Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 Premium Version

Premium users enjoy:

  • Completely ad-free experience
  • No ad tracking or data collection
  • One-time purchase (£3.99)

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • View: Access all your stored data within the app
  • Edit: Modify your information at any time
  • Delete: Remove students, classes, or lessons
  • Export: Download your data as PDF or CSV

6.2 Notification Control

You can:

  • Enable or disable notifications in the app
  • Customize which notifications you receive
  • Manage notification permissions in iOS Settings

6.3 Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  • Contact us at smassservice@gmail.com
  • We will permanently delete your account and associated data
  • This action cannot be undone

7. Children's Privacy

SSmanager is designed for use by adults (teachers, administrators, and parents) to manage Sunday School programs. While the app stores information about children (students), it is not directed at children under 13.

Important:

  • Only adults should create accounts and use the app
  • Adults are responsible for the student data they enter
  • We do not knowingly collect information directly from children
  • Parents have the right to request deletion of their child's information

8. International Users

SSmanager uses Firebase, which stores data in Google's global infrastructure:

  • Your data may be stored in data centers outside your country
  • Google complies with international data protection laws
  • Data transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards

9. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Active Accounts: As long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backups: May persist in backups for up to 90 days
  • Legal Requirements: Longer if required by law
